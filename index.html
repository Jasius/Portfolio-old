About me

Hi, my name is Mindaugas Jasiulis, I am years old self-taught Front-end developer. Currently working on part time projects and maintaining Reddit community forum during my free time.

Best experience to me as a junior Front-end developer was on Reddit community forum /r/Windows10 simply due to its big audience which allowed me to see how effective some of the Front-end techniques such as color coding are. Content structuring, color coding helped content engagement and interaction grow significantly. Im happy that my work reaches over 1 million unique users every month on Windows community alone while on /r/Olympics community during 2016 Olympic games my work was seen by 3 million unique users and viewed 22 million times. I've received job offers from people that loved my work on Windows Community and other communities also thanks to this side project I have had plenty of interaction with Microsoft engineers, prominent developers and lots of other interesting people.

Apart from Web development related work i was involved in translating websites such as OneFootball into Lithuanian and Russian languages.

Projects

Windows Community

2016

I was invited to redesign this subreddit, I enjoy and use software that Microsoft makes and because of that i couldn't pass this opportunity to present my skills to such a large amount of people and i spent quite a bit of my free time discussing things on Windows 10 subreddit anyways. My main goal on this community forum was to increase community interaction and come up with a way to highlight certain content which people enjoy. Bookie.GG

2015

Bookie.GG is a gambling site on which you were able to bet Counter-Strike: Global Offensive weapon skins, which can be traded, sold on Steam market or third party services. CS GO skins hold monetary value on the market, so betting took off really quickly once skins were introduced into the game.
My role on this project was supporting Front-end developer, community manager.

Invisibars extension

2017

Invisibars is a Chrome extension which removes wasteful scrolls bars to give you more real estate while browsing the web. It is a simple extension with very low footprint and unlike other similar extensions Invisibars does not break custom modals, scrollbars on websites.

ClearShift

2015

ClearShift is a site for promoting my third major version of my Reddit theme which I originally made for /r/MicrosoftBand community, I put a lot of hours into it, so I wanted to out source it and make it easy to customize, set up with pre-set code snippets so even people with little to no CSS knowledge can use ClearShift and customize it to their needs. Also broadening the reach of my ClearShift theme helps me to improve it even further due to feedback and bug reports. Also im using this site to promote other subreddits that use ClearShift so people can see how it looks in action.

During 2016 Olympic games my theme was being used on /r/Olympics community so my theme was seen by 3 million unique users and was viewed 22 million times during 2016 Olympics month.
